Founder

Juanita Jones

Juanita is the Executive Director and founder of Keep My Hood Good Community Campaign. Upon high school graduation, she studied Psychology and Criminal Justice at University of Tennessee at Martin. Juanita is currently a member of the Capt Jack Holland Detachment #735 United States Marine Corps League in Jackson. She graduated from Leadership Jackson in 2001. In 2009 Juanita founded Keep My Hood Good to serve the children of Jackson who are stuck in generational poverty. What started as playing kickball in Lincoln Courts and Parkway East has turned into a nourshing family and educational environment for students from local schools to learn and demostrate the principles of accountability, excellence, integrity and respect of self and to others.
